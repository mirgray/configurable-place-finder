Online Help:

The Wildlife Management Area Locator application helps citizens locate a WMA and obtain information about activities within their community. This application is typically used by residents within a State, but can be used by visitors to locate a wildlife anagemet area near them, or one that offers the activity they are interested in.

To locate a WMA simply enter an address, or WMA name in the search box or select activities. Or, you can use your current location to find a WMA near you. The WMA(s) will then be highlighted on the map and relevant information about available activities presented to you.

Using the Application

The Wildlife Management Area Locator application provides residents and visitors an easy way to find WMAs near them, a WMA by it's name, or recreation opportunities offered in areas within the State. Enter your address or click on the Current Location button to find WMAs near you. If you click the Current Location button, it will use the location of your computer's IP address or your cell phone/tablet location to locate WMAs near you. When you enter an address or use your current location, the application will present a list of WMAs near you and allow you to review relevant information about each WMA. In addition, you can search for a WMA, or specific recreation activity you'd like to undertake, by name. To do that, enter the name of the WMA in the search box. When you enter the name of the WMA, the application will find the WMA or present a list of WMAs that offer the recreation activity you're interested in and allow you to review relevant information about each WMA.

Finding an Area of Interest

There are three primary ways to identify an area of interest. You can interact directly with the map and click on a WMA when you've located it. You can also use your current location or an address to find a WMA near you. Finally, you can search for a WMA by name.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Use the basemap switcher to select the basemap you'd like to interact with
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Click the + or - to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address or WMA and recreation area you're interested in:

1. Enter the address or WMA name you're interested in
2. Click on the item in the list to locate the address or click on the WMA in the list on the left panel

You can enter a partial address and then identify the correct value in the list.

The Activities panel can be used to search for WMAs by recrational activities:

1. Click on each activity you are intersted in and click search.

Recreation Activities

When you find a WMA you're interested in, you can select it from the list and it will then be highlighted on the map. Additional detail about each WMA and the recreational activities available will be provided in the information panel. When you click on an individual WMA on the map, the same information will be displayed.

Directions

You can also get directions to each WMA using your current location or an address you select. When you find a WMA you'd like to visit, enter an address to start from, or click the use current location button, to calculate the directions and route.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share Map button to share your map several different ways. You can compose an email, post to your Facebook wall or post a Tweet. When you use the Facebook or Twitter links, you'll be asked to sign in to your respective account.

More Information

The Wildlife Management Area Locator application can used by State Fish and Wildlife, State DNR, State Tourism or other State government organizations responsible for managing WMAs to deploy a web-based WMA locator application. This application provides 24x7 access to the organization and typically supplements customer service phone numbers staffed by these organizations.

Please visit Esri's ArcGIS for State Government Resource Center for additional information.
